## Prefix Sum (누적합)

$$O(n*m)\Longrightarrow O(n)$$

| 일반 (A): | 6 | 3|-2| 4|-1|0|5|
|Prefix Sum Array: | 6|9|7|11|10|10|5|

- `PSA[ i ] = PSA[ i - 1 ] + A[ i ]`
- Calculate `[ 2 , 6 ]` $$\Rightarrow$$ `[ 0 , 6 ]` - `[ 0 , 1 ]`

- Calculating Prefix Sum Array: $$O(n)$$
- Perform Range Sum : $$O(1)$$
